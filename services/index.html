Product line 01

Pumps & Valves

Excel Trade International Ltd proudly offers a comprehensive range of high-quality pumps and valves, perfect for a multitude of applications, including water supply, wastewater treatment, and industrial processes. Our partnerships with top-tier manufacturers ensure our products are renowned for their exceptional durability and efficiency.

  • Centrifugal, submersible, and positive displacement pumps
  • Gate, globe, ball, and butterfly valves
  • Products sourced from internationally recognised manufacturers

Typical applications

Water supply infrastructure, wastewater treatment plants, oil & gas processing facilities, and general industrial pipework.

Product line 02

Motors & Drives

Excel Trade International Limited is your trusted partner for high-quality motors and drives, essential components for a wide range of industrial and commercial applications. Our products are designed to meet the demanding needs of modern industry, offering reliability, efficiency, and superior performance.

  • AC and DC electric motors for industrial use
  • Variable frequency drives (VFDs) and soft starters
  • Designed for reliability, efficiency, and superior performance

Typical applications

Pump and compressor drive systems, conveyor systems, HVAC, and process automation across mining and petrochemical facilities.

Product line 03

Electrical & Automation Solutions

Excel Trade International Ltd excels in the design, installation, and servicing of advanced electrical control panel boards and automation systems. We provide tailored solutions to meet the specific requirements of both residential and industrial clients, ensuring seamless integration and operational excellence.

  • Custom electrical control panel design and build
  • Automation system integration and commissioning
  • Ongoing servicing and technical support

Typical applications

Industrial process control, motor control centres (MCCs), building management systems, and facility automation.

Specialised Equipment

  • Compliant with international engineering standards

Municipal water treatment, industrial fluid systems, and oil & gas pipeline infrastructure.
